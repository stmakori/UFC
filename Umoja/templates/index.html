Key Features

Everything you need to connect farmers with brokers

Route Management

Brokers can create and manage delivery routes with multiple stops, optimizing their collection and delivery operations.

Easy Booking

Farmers can search for available routes and book space for their produce with just a few clicks.

Real-time Tracking

Track your shipments in real-time and receive updates on delivery status and estimated arrival times.

Secure Payments

Integrated payment system ensures secure transactions between farmers and brokers.

Analytics Dashboard

Comprehensive analytics and reporting tools to track performance and make data-driven decisions.

Community Network

Connect with a network of verified farmers and brokers for reliable business partnerships.
