About Umoja

Revolutionizing agricultural logistics in Kenya through innovative freight consolidation solutions that connect farmers directly with reliable transport brokers.

Our Mission

To empower smallholder farmers and freight brokers with a seamless platform that reduces transportation costs, minimizes food waste, and ensures timely delivery of agricultural produce to markets.

Our Vision

To become East Africa's leading agricultural logistics platform, creating a transparent, efficient, and sustainable supply chain that benefits all stakeholders in the agricultural ecosystem.

Our Story

Umoja was born from a simple observation: smallholder farmers in Kenya were losing up to 40% of their income to transportation costs, while freight brokers were running half-empty trucks on the same routes.

Founded in 2024, we set out to solve this problem by creating a digital platform that connects farmers with freight brokers, enabling them to consolidate shipments, share transportation costs, and optimize delivery routes.

Today, Umoja serves over 500 farmers and 150 brokers across Kenya, facilitating thousands of deliveries and saving farmers millions of shillings in transportation costs.
